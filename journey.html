Week 01

Journey

The shift from MDCAT ambition to engineering commitment.

This page organizes the transition into a clearer timeline: the original ambition, the narrow miss, and the serious decision to move forward in computer engineering.

Guiding Line

A changed path does not erase ambition. It can refine it into something stronger and more suitable.

Timeline

Three stages in the academic transition.

01

MDCAT-first focus

The original ambition was medicine, and preparation demanded full attention. That intensity built discipline, but it also made everything feel dependent on one outcome.

02

A narrow miss

Missing admission by a very small margin brought frustration, comparison, and a difficult emotional reset. It also forced a more realistic view of the future.

03

A serious engineering commitment

Computer engineering becomes meaningful when it is chosen with intention, backed by skill-building, practical work, and a forward-looking mindset.

What Changes Now
  • The future is no longer defined by one exam.
  • The focus shifts from uncertainty to practical skill.
  • Weekly reflection becomes part of the growth process.
Guiding Line

Success is not confined to one path. It can also be built through adjustment, maturity, and commitment to a better fit.
